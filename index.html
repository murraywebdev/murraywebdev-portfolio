About Me

I'm Rach! A little bit about me...

I believe that doing work that is meaningful to both the creator and the recipient of the creation allows the evolution of a thriving project. In the world of development and design, specifically for the web, nothing is set in stone. Websites live, breathe and change all the time. That’s what makes them so incredible and fun to work with!

A liberal arts education combined with professional experience on the business side and a self-invested coding background puts me in a unique position at this point in my life. A path of seemingly random experiences has led me here, and I can’t wait to see what’s next.

My favorite part about what I do is coding websites from scratch but I also love: photography, graphic design, branding, SEO, advertising, print design, and getting to know new people!
